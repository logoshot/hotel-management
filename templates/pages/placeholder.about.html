On this page, you can learn more about Jumeirah Sailing Hotel.

Jumeirah Sailing Hotel ( Arabic : برج العرب ) , also known as the Sailing Hotel , Arabian Tower, or Transylvania Hotel , is a luxurious hotel located in Dubai , United Arab Emirates . It stands 321 meters high and stands on the beach. The artificial island in the Persian Gulf , 280 meters away from the shore, is connected to the land by a curved road. Managed by Jumeirah Luxury Hotels Group .

The hotel was built in 1994 and opened to the public in December 1999. Its appearance resembles a dhow (an Arabian ship), and it was intentionally built in a place that will not let the shadow of the building cover the beach. The top of the restaurant is equipped with a cantilever structure with a cantilever structure protruding from the edge of the building.

The characteristic of the Arab Tower is "high". The atrium lobby is 180 meters high or 590 feet high. The 38-story atrium also contains the Dubai World Trade Center , the tallest building in Dubai in the late 1970s and mid-1990s.
